Detox FAQs

How long does detox take?
Detox generally takes 3-7 days, depending on individual circumstances.
Is detox painful?
Detox can be uncomfortable, but our team provides medications to ease symptoms and make the process as comfortable as possible.
What happens after detox?
After detox, we offer therapy and support programs to help you stay on track with your recovery.
Do you offer dual diagnosis treatment?
Yes, we treat co-occurring mental health issues alongside addiction.
