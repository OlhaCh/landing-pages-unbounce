Frequently Asked Questions

What types of conditions do you treat?

We treat a wide range of mental health conditions and substance use disorders, including depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, PTSD, and addiction to drugs or alcohol.

What is the duration of the treatment programs?

Treatment duration varies based on individual needs, typically ranging from 30 to 90 days, with some requiring longer stays.

What is included in the treatment program?

Our programs include individual and group therapy, holistic therapies, medical support, family therapy, and aftercare planning.

How does the admissions process work?

The process starts with a confidential assessment, followed by insurance verification and admission scheduling.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept various insurance plans and offer financial assistance for those who qualify.

What is a typical day like in the inpatient program?

A typical day includes therapy sessions, holistic activities, meals, and recreational activities.

Is family involvement part of the treatment?

Yes, we offer family therapy sessions to help repair and strengthen family relationships.

What kind of support is available after they leave the program?

We provide aftercare planning and support to help you maintain your recovery.

How do I know if inpatient treatment is right for them?

Inpatient treatment is ideal for those needing intensive, round-the-clock support in a structured environment.

How can I contact you if I have more questions?

Reach out via our contact form, call our admissions hotline, or email us directly.
