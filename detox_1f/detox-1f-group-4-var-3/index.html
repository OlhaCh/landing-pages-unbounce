Detox FAQs

  • Is detox painful?

    Detox can be uncomfortable, but we provide medications to ease symptoms and keep you as comfortable as possible.

  • How long does detox take?

    Detox typically lasts between 3-7 days, depending on the substance and individual.

  • Will I be monitored during detox?

    Yes, we provide 24/7 medical supervision to ensure your safety throughout the process.

  • What happens after detox?

    After detox, we offer therapy and continued support to help you maintain sobriety.
