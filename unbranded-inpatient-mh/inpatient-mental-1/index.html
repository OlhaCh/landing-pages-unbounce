Taking Care of Your Mental Health Matters

Struggling with depression, anxiety, or other mental health challenges can feel overwhelming. You don't have to go through this alone. We offer comprehensive inpatient and outpatient treatment programs designed to help you find healing and create a path towards a brighter future.

Why Choose Us for Your Mental Health Journey?

We provide individualized treatment plans delivered by a team of experienced and compassionate professionals.

What to Expect From Our Inpatient and Outpatient Programs

Our inpatient program offers 24/7 care in a safe and secure environment, ideal for those needing intensive support. Our outpatient program provides flexible scheduling for ongoing therapy and support while living at home.

Find the Right Level of Care

We understand that everyone's needs are different. This table compares the key features of our inpatient and outpatient programs to help you choose the best fit.

SL Feature Inpatient Treatment Outpatient Treatment
1. Level of Support 24/7 supervision and medical care Flexible scheduling, with regular therapy sessions
2. Living Arrangements Live at the treatment facility Live at home
3. Program Duration Varies, typically 3-7 days to several weeks Varies, can be ongoing or short-term
4. Ideal For Individuals in crisis, needing Intensive stabilization, or with high-risk factors Individuals with mild to moderate symptoms who can function at home and attend appointments

Frequently Asked Questions

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept a variety of insurance plans. Please contact our admissions team to verify coverage for your specific plan.

What are the qualifications of your therapists?

Answer: All our therapists are licensed mental health professionals with extensive experience treating various mental health conditions.

What happens during a consultation?

A consultation is a free and confidential meeting to discuss your needs and goals for treatment. We will assess your situation and recommend the most appropriate program for you.

Is medication-assisted treatment available?

Yes, medication-assisted treatment (MAT) may be integrated into your treatment plan if deemed necessary by a qualified healthcare professional.

What happens after I complete the program?

We offer aftercare planning to help you transition back to daily life and maintain your progress. This may include recommendations for ongoing therapy, support groups, or other resources.
