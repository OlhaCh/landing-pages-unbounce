What to Expect From Our Inpatient and Outpatient Programs

Our inpatient program offers 24/7 care in a safe and secure environment, ideal for those needing intensive support. Our outpatient program provides flexible scheduling for ongoing therapy and support while living at home.

Find the Right Level of Care

We understand that everyone's needs are different. This table compares the key features of our inpatient and outpatient programs to help you choose the best fit.

Feature Inpatient Treatment Outpatient Treatment
Level of Support 24/7 supervision and medical care Flexible scheduling, with regular therapy sessions
Living Arrangements Live at the treatment facility Live at home

Frequently Asked Questions

We understand you might have questions about our mental health treatment program. Here are some of the most common inquiries we receive.

What is the length of the mental health treatment program?
The length of stay varies depending on individual needs but typically ranges from 30 to 90 days.
Will I be able to contact my family during treatment?
Yes, we encourage family involvement and provide scheduled times for family visits and communication
What types of therapies are offered?
We offer a variety of therapies including individual counseling, group therapy, cognitive-behavioral therapy, art therapy, and mindfulness practices.
