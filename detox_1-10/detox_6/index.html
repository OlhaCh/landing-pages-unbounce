Frequently Asked
Detox Questions

Is detox painful?
We use medications to help manage withdrawal symptoms and make the process as comfortable as possible.
Can I leave detox early?
Leaving early is not recommended, as it can be dangerous to stop detox prematurely.
Do you offer mental health support?
Yes, we provide dual diagnosis treatment for those with co-occurring mental health conditions.
